Interactive RTA Prediction Map

This interactive map allows you to find accident hotspots along an optimized driving route between two locations in Greater London at a particular time.

To use the map, please pick :
(1) a date/time in the next 48 hours,
(2) your origin and
(3) your destination

Your inputs will result in an API call to Google Map's Geolocation API, which will select the best driving route connecting your origin and your destination. This route will be shown on the map. In addition, your inputs will also trigger an API call to Dark Sky, which will provide information about the weather condition in London for your input time. We will then feed all these inputs to our predictive model, which will output the corresponding RTA risk on the map.

Refresh page if there are no dropdown boxes in the map. Please note that accident points may take a while to load.
